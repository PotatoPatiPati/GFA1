Advocacate for those affected by Earthquakes.

Between 1998-2017, earthquakes caused nearly 750 000 deaths globally, more than half of all deaths related to natural disasters.

More than 125 million people were affected by earthquakes during this time, meaning they were injured, lost homes, displaced or evacuated during the emergency phase of the disaster.

Agriculture and nature have been severely affected by earthquakes, leading to the broken homes of humans and animals.

Help us in advocating for those who lost their homes and their loved ones because of natural disasters.
